Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

OnCall is a shift coverage marketplace for nurses at St. Elizabeth's Hospital. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information we collect

Account information

When you sign up, we collect your hospital email address, full name, and department or specialty. Your email must be a verified St. Elizabeth's Hospital address.

Profile information

Your name and department as provided when setting up or editing your profile. If you choose to set a profile picture, we access the photo you select from your photo library and store it so it can be displayed to other verified users. Providing a profile picture is optional.

Shift and transaction data

Shifts you post, shifts you claim, offer amounts, and transaction history within the app.

Payment information

We use Stripe to process payments. We do not store your card number, bank account details, or other sensitive financial data directly — this is handled entirely by Stripe. We store your Stripe account identifiers to facilitate payouts between nurses.

Usage data

Basic app usage information used to maintain and improve the service.

How we use your information

  • To operate the shift coverage marketplace
  • To process payments between nurses via Stripe
  • To send transactional emails such as account confirmation and shift notifications
  • To display your name and department to other verified nurses at St. Elizabeth's Hospital

Who we share your information with

Stripe

Payment processing and payout management. Stripe's privacy policy applies to information they collect during payment processing and Connect onboarding. See stripe.com/privacy.

Supabase

Database and authentication infrastructure. Your data is stored securely on Supabase's servers. See supabase.com/privacy.

Other nurses at St. Elizabeth's Hospital

Your name, department, and shift activity are visible to other verified users of the app. Offer amounts on shift posts are visible to all verified users.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with advertisers.

Data retention and account deletion

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Shift and transaction records are retained for accounting and dispute resolution purposes. You may delete your account at any time from within the app (Profile → Delete account) or by contacting us at the email below. When you delete your account, your profile and personal information are removed; anonymized transaction records may be retained where required for accounting and legal purposes.

Your rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data

To make any of these requests, contact us using the email below.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), row-level security on our database, and Stripe's PCI-compliant payment infrastructure.

Children's privacy

OnCall is intended for licensed nurses and medical staff only. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify active users of significant changes via email.
